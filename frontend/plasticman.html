POLYBAG DIVISION:

This division produces both PP and PE Polybag. We have 4 machines to produces Polybag. Two of them are PP and other two are PE Poly producers. Our Polybag Printer can print 4 colors at a time. We have all other necessary machineries like Side sealing and Bottom Cutting machines to give the Poly division a complete self dependent shape. Moreover we are going to setup new machineries under expansion program.

HANGER DIVISION:

The Hanger division is equipped with modern injection Moulding Machines that produce various types of Hangers. Among them Suit / Jacket Hanger, Trouser Hanger, Knitwear Hanger, Dress/ Blouse/ Shirt Hanger are most prominent.

We use GPPS,HIPS, PP, K-resin and a wide range of colors matching pigments to manufacture any Hanger of your requirement. Many of our products can be produced with unique logo of customers; either by embossing or by placing adhesive sticker of different colors. We have committed for the environment. That is why we are recycling rejected or damaged hangers to decrease the production of plastic waste. We are one of the fastest growing manufacturers conducting operations with high zeal to be the market leader.

SEWING THREAD DIVISION:

We have Rolling Machines, Fiber Machines and Sewing thread winder (i.e. Coning Machine) made in China. We produce high quality thread as per approved sample and deliver the order quantity within shortest duration. We produce 100% spun polyester sewing threads of all counts, that is 50/2, 40/2, 60/2, 20/2, 20/3, 20/9.

EMBROIDERY DIVISION

We can do all sorts of embroidery on shirt, pant, cap, knit wear, Belt of shoe, Sticker, Shari etc. We can also make discs of embroidery for punching as per your design. We have 2 ZSK machine made in Germany containing 20 heads each that can perform 9 colors at a time. This machine is considering best type of machine in Bangladesh in terms of quality output.

PRINTING DIVISION

We do Screen Printing on jacket, Knitted items, Cartoons and Labels. More specifically to say, we conduct Taffeta print Rubber print. Currently we have four printing tables, each with 60 feet length. We are in process to set up more printing tables to expand our production capacity. We can produce the accurate design through our own laboratory.
